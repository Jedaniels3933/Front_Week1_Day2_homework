Overlord Overview

Isekai is a Japanese genre of fantasy that involves a main character being transported to a new world, either physically, mentally, or through reincarnation. The word "isekai" literally translates to "different world" in Japanese. The character may have to survive in the unfamiliar setting, find their way home, or live a regular life. What makes this story great is that the main character is over powered in this world and uses that advantage to eventually rule over the human population ruthlessly. Even though the main character Ainz Oaal Gown is basically an evil monster that slaughters humans with no hesitation, the author makes you love the guy because he is so nerdy, awkward and relatable.


Characters

Ainz Ooal Gown

Ainz Ooal Gown is the main protagonist of the Overlord series. He is the guild master of Ainz Ooal Gown, Overlord of the Great Tomb of Nazarick, and the creator of Pandora's Actor. He is regarded as the highest of the Almighty 41 Supreme Beings by the NPCs of Nazarick. He is a skeleton mage that is the ruler of the Great Tomb of Nazarick. He is the most powerful being in the world and is the Sorcerer King of the Sorcerer Kingdom.


The Floor Guardians

The floor Guardians are a group of NPCs who act as the bosses of Nazarick's floors, having gained great influence over its affairs after coming to the New World and gaining sentience. They are among the most powerful beings in Nazarick, with some exceeding Ainz in stats. They are absolutely loyal to Ainz and allow him to control every thing that they do as well as follow any order he gives.


Manga Light Novels

Overlord (Japanese: オーバーロード, Hepburn: Ōbārōdo)

Overlord is a Japanese light novel series written by Kugane Maruyama and illustrated by so-bin. It began serialization online in 2010, before being acquired by Enterbrain. Sixteen volumes have been published since July 2012. A manga adaptation by Satoshi Ōshio, with art by Hugin Miyama, began serialization in Kadokawa Shoten's manga magazine Comp Ace from November 26, 2014. Both the light novels and the manga are licensed in North America by Yen Press since 2016.
